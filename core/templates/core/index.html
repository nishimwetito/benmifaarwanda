About Benjamin

Benjamin Nkusi is a remarkable Rwandan educator, writer, and public servant who has dedicated his life to empowering communities through education, literature, and sustainable development initiatives.

As an educator with over 20 years of experience, Benjamin has taught at various schools and universities, inspiring countless students with his passion for learning and commitment to excellence. His innovative teaching methods have been recognized nationally, earning him the Rwandan Excellence in Education Award in 2018.

As a writer, Benjamin has authored seven acclaimed books and numerous articles that explore themes of reconciliation, community development, and Rwandan heritage. His memoir "From the Ashes of History" has become required reading in several African studies programs.
